Eclipse Charcuterie

Savory Shadows on a Wooden Canvas

The Eclipse Charcuterie is a masterpiece of contrasts, where bold flavors and muted tones collide. House-cured shadow meats, aged midnight cheeses, and obsidian olives are arranged in an artful display, each element chosen for its depth and intrigue. A tart inkberry compote adds a touch of unexpected sweetness, balancing the rich umami with a flicker of brightness. Designed for those who appreciate the interplay of flavors and textures, this dish is a slow exploration of the night’s bounty, best shared over candlelight and hushed conversation.
