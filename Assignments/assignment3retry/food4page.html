Veiled Venison

Wrapped in Mystery, Unveiled with Every Bite

At the heart of the Veiled Venison lies a story of contrast—tender venison medallions, seared to perfection, encased in caramelized cabbage leaves that conceal their richness. The deep, robust flavor of the meat is complemented by a dark cherry reduction, its slight tartness enhancing the dish’s natural complexity. Wisps of black radish add a final layer of depth, ensuring that every forkful is an elegant revelation. Like a hidden passageway in an unfamiliar city, this dish is both elusive and unforgettable.
