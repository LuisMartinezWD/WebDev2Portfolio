Phantom’s Harvest Risotto

A Golden Glow in the Darkened Hollow

A dish that thrives in both darkness and light, the Phantom’s Harvest Risotto is an ethereal blend of saffron-infused Arborio rice, activated charcoal, and wild mushrooms harvested from deep within the forest. The result is a stunning interplay of golden hues against an obsidian backdrop, a visual echo of the duality of night and day. Crowned with a golden yolk that melts seamlessly into the dish, this risotto offers a luscious, creamy texture and an unmistakable depth of flavor. A reminder that even in the blackest night, warmth and richness can still be found.
